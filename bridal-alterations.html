About This Service

Bridal Gown Alterations in Sacramento

Finding the perfect wedding gown is only half the journey. The other half is making sure it fits you — your actual body, your posture, your movement — with the kind of precision that only comes from a skilled hand and a trained eye.

Carla Westerbeck has been altering bridal gowns for over 24 years and has worked on everything from simple hem adjustments to complete bodice reconstructions. Your first appointment is always a free consultation where Carla assesses the gown, discusses your vision, and provides a full quote.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?
Three to four months before your wedding date is ideal. Carla will always do her best to accommodate shorter timelines when possible.
Do you charge a fitting fee?
No. Your initial consultation is always free. Carla will assess your gown and provide a full quote before anything begins.
What should I bring to my first appointment?
Bring your gown, your shoes if you have them, and any shapewear or undergarments you plan to wear on the day.
How many fittings will I need?
Most brides require two to three fittings depending on the complexity of the alterations.
